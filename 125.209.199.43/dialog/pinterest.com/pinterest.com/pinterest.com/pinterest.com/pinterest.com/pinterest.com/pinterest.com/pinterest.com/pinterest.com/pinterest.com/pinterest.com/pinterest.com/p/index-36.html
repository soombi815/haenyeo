The birthplace of Haenyeo is thought to be Jeju Island. HAENYEO is a specialty of the sea of Jeju. They work under the sea at a depth of 5m for around 30 seconds. They can even stand for two minutes or longer under water at a depth of 20m when necessary. Demonstrating the strength and tenacity of the women on Jeju Island, the Haenyeo diving performance is a special attraction that can only be experienced at Aqua Planet.

Haenam who loved the life of Haenyeo

Soombi Inc. originated from a love for Haenyeo will strive not only for preservation of Haenyeo culture, but for promoting it as Korea’s representative contents and ultimately a world cultural heritage.

WHO

WE ARE

I entered Jeju Hansupul Haenyeo School to become a Haenam in 2009 as I had always yearned for the life of Jeju despite my presence in Seoul. Here at Haenyeo School, I was so impressed with strong-minded Jeju Haenyeo who live their life next to the sea to raise their family. I was also encountered with possibility that Hanyeo tradition may vanish from our memories as it discontinues to the next generations. There, I formed a non-profit organization, Jeju Haenyeo Culture Preservation Association.

Traditional Haenyeo Harvest Performance & HAENYEO DINNING

Traditional Haenyeo harvest performance is demonstrated by 20 representative Haenyeo of Gosung Sinyang fishing village. Experienced Haenyeo dive into 20m deep water pool and perform their daily harvest life to audiences.
The performance is held four times a day all year around with 500,000 accumulated visitors.
